crunch it! is an annoying browser game where time is inconsiderate and the work of the player goes nowhere. Featuring a handmade website and an original soundtrack, the game satirizes my relationship with work and constant quest for temporary, fleeting feelings of satisfaction through it. The game and the soundtrack are both free to play/listen to.
