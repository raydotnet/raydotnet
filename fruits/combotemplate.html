Fruits of a Handmade Web
link
Fruits of a Handmade Web is a companion piece to Suspicious Apple Gallery and was designed with both the form of a cascading webpage and an accordion book in mind. The work contains excerpts from The Complete Book of Fruit : an illustrated guide to over 400 species and varieties of fruit from all over the world (by Dick Pijpers, Jac. G. Constant, and Kees Jansen), My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be? (by Laurel Schwulst), and the code itself.
